HOW IT WORKS?

Access to Information and Privacy (ATIP) grants individuals the right to access records held by government institutions, including personal information. For instance, if your Canadian visa application was refused, ATIP can help you understand the reasons behind the refusal and formulate a new strategy for reapplication.

Government institutions are required to respond to access to information or personal information requests within 30 days. Providing a specific and detailed request can facilitate a quicker response from the institution.
